Manchester Compiled Tech

We are a group of individuals, communities and companies, that have committed a pledge to be inclusive and participatory in the wider Manchester Tech community.

Find participants below and if you wish to join the CompiledMCR group, check out or GitHub and add “part of @CompiledMCR” in your Twitter bio.
